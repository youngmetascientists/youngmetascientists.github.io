Are you interested in contributing to the Platform for Young Meta-Scientists?

One of our board members, Sajedeh, is stepping down, and we are looking for someone to join the board and take on this role.

**What does the position involve?**

- Keeping an overview of PYMS activities and the division of tasks
- Scheduling, preparing agendas for, and moderating board meetings
- Acting as a liaison between PYMS and relevant initiatives
- Updating the website
- Supporting the planning and execution of PYMS events

On average, the role requires about 1 hour every two weeks. The workload may vary, with quieter periods and busier times around events.

**Who are we looking for?**

We are looking for someone who:

- Has a collaborative, team-oriented mindset
- Can commit for at least 2 years
- Has an interest in meta-science and related topics
- Is currently (or soon to be) in a research role in meta-science for the next 2 years (e.g., PhD, postdoc, research assistant), or in a position closely connected to researchers
